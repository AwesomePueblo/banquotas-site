What We Do

Every job handled by experienced operators with commercial-grade equipment.

🪓

Stump Grinding

We grind stumps 6–12 inches below grade so grass can grow back over them. All sizes handled—from saplings to century-old oaks.

🌱

Root Removal

Surface roots tearing up your lawn or driveway? We can cut and remove root systems to protect your hardscape.

🧹

Chip & Debris Cleanup

Ground chips are raked back into the hole or bagged and hauled away—your choice. We leave your yard tidy.

📋

Free Estimates

On-site estimates at no charge. We measure the stump, assess access, and give you a firm price before any work begins.

🏘️

Multi-Stump Discounts

Have several stumps? We price jobs in bulk—the more stumps, the lower the per-stump cost. Ask about our volume rate.

🚚

Emergency Removal

Storm damage or hazard stumps? We offer priority scheduling and can often arrive within 24–48 hours.
